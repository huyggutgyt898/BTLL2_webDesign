eCommerce

Ecommerce or electronic commerce, a subset of ebusiness, e-commerce is the purchasing and exchanging of goods and services over computer networks (such as the internet) through which transactins or terms of sale are performed electronically.

class="list-heading">Ecommerce can be broken into four main categories

  • B2C (Business-to-Consumer)
  • C2B (Consumer-to-Business)
  • C2C (Consumer-to-Consumer)
