INDIAN ELEHANT

The Indian elephant (Elephas maximus inducus) is one of three recognied subpecies of the Asian elephant and native to mainland Aisa.
Since 1986,Elephas maximus has been listed as Endangered by IUCN as the population has declined by at least 50% over the last
three generatioons , estimated to be 60-75 year. Asian elephants are threatened by habits loss, degradation and fragmentation.
